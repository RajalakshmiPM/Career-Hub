3. So after engineering you will be having 2 options either you can prepare for campus placements or to any other dream company or else you can do master's.

  1. If you want to get into good company, you have to prepare for interview rounds/tests like quantitative aptitude, technical aptitude, GD and other general soft skills. For service based companies if you get ready for these stuff its good enough. For some companies technical aptitude is excluded.

    Visit www.sahicareer.com for online aptitude test

  2. If you are aiming for product based companies like Google, Microsoft etc must prepare for Gate, since most of the question that appear in technical aptitude/test will be of Gate exams based.

    Visit www.indiabix.com for all details and interview questions regarding Product based companines

  3. If you would like to continue your studies and willing to choose ME/MTech again you have to prepare for Gate. If you're willing to get into reputational college your Gate scores must be good.

    Visit gate.iitm.ac.in for all details regarding Gate exams

  4. MS@UA, Canada, etc this will be your other option if you are willing to do study abroad and for this you have to learn their communial languages like french, german and so on.. it completely depends on your GRE,GMAT scores

    Visit studyabroad.shiksha.com for all details regarding Colleges and fees to study in abroad

  5. MBA is basically for people who are more interested in maganement jobs. Again it is well and good if you're doing in reputated college and the exams that need to be prepared for this degree would be CAT, MAT, PGCET, XMAT, and so on...

    Visit edu.byjus.com for all details regarding MBA exams, colleges and placements
